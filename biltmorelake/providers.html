Understanding the Chart

Fiber to the node - fiber cable to a neighborhood distribution point, may or may not be to the residence.
Fiber to the residence - fiber cable to your home, typically meaning a dedicated fiber strand to ensure consistent speed.
Satellite - signal from website is from satellite to a receiving dish near your home.
DSL - digital subscriber line with internet provided by copper cable.
Cable - copper lines that historically were used for cable TV transmission but upgraded with new technology including fiber to local nodes.
Fixed wireless - direct transmission from mobile cell tower to home computer through a receiver on the residence.
Wireless - transmission from mobile cell tower to home computer.

For more information on Broadband Types, see Broadband 101.
